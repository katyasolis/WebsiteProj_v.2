History of the Camera:

1816 - The first camera was invented by French inventor, Nicephor Niepce.
1839 - The Daguerrotype , an early form of the camera was invented by, Louis Daguerre.
1850 - The Daguerrotype was replaced by a new "colloid process".
1888 - The first Roll Film camera was used called "The Kodak".
1934 - The kodak camera company introduced 135 film and it quickly became the standard.
1978 - The first Auto-focus camera.
1990 - The first Digital Manipulation Software arrived on personal computers.
1999 - The first Phone Camera was the Kyocera VP-210.
